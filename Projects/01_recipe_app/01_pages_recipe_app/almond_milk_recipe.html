BASE INGREDIENTS YOU NEED TO MAKE ALMOND MILK

  • 40g Almond
  • 4 Cups water

HOW TO MAKE A BASIC Almond MILK

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine raw almonds with enough water to cover the almonds by at least three inches.
  3. Let stand overnight.
  4. The next day, strain the almonds and discard the soaking water. Then transfer to a high-speed blender with the 4 cups of filtered water and a pinch of salt.
  5. Blend until smooth and well blended, about 1 minute. Be careful not to run the blender or food processor too long as this will heat the milk.
  6. If more blending is required, stop, wait, for milk to cool, and then blend again.
  7. Strain the milk through a nut milk bag, paper coffee filter, or a clean and thin dish towel.
  8. Pour the almond milk into a bottle, seal, and place in the coldest place in your refrigerator for up to 7 days.

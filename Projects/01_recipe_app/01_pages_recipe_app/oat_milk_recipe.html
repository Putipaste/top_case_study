BASE INGREDIENTS YOU NEED TO MAKE OAT MILK

  • 40g Oats
  • 1 Cup water

HOW TO MAKE A BASIC OAT MILK

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Place the oats and salt in a high-speed blender.
  3. Add the salt and combine all ingredients together.
  4. Look for a nice milkiness but do not expect every speck of oats to be incorporated into the liquid.
  5. If you really need to blend more, wait a minute or two for the second round of pulses so as not to heat the milk with the power of the blender. You do not want to start cooking the oats as this creates a gunky texture.

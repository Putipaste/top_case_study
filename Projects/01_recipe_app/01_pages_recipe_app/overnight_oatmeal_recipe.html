BASE INGREDIENTS YOU NEED TO MAKE OVERNIGHT OATS

  • 40g Oats
  • 1 Cup Milk
  • 10g Chia seeds (optional)
  • 1/2 Cup Greek or vegan yogurt (optional)
  • 1 drop Vanilla extract (optional)
  • Sweetener (optional)
  • Toppings

HOW TO MAKE A BASIC OVERNIGHT OATS RECIPE

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine milk, yogurt, oats, honey, and chia seeds in a 1/2-pint jar with a lid; cover and shake until combined.
  3. Fold in toppins.
  4. Cover and refrigerate, 8 hours to overnight. Enjoy!
